Visit Patna

Enjoy culture and sports in this vibrant district which is capital of Bihar.

Top three activities to do at Patna

Attend a show or a concert

The city centre is a great place to spend your night out.

Go for a picnic in the local park

The park is full of green areas with more than 3,000 varieties of plants.

Play sports at the Gandhi Maidan

Play volleyball, football, basket ball or lift some weights.
